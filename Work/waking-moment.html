WAKING MOMENT

Next: Just For Now
Previous: Eurostile Specimen

An experimental short film exploring fleeting moments of wakefulness - decisive moments of clarity that vanish almost immediately. Inspired by excerpt from Jun'ichirō Tanizaki's In Praise of Shadows (1933): “I need only pause before it and I forget the passage of time.” Frames include both digital video and 35mm film, collaged and scanned. Sound includes found audio and instrumental from Sour Times by Portishead.
