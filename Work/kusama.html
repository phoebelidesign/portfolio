An experimental poster collage for Yayoi Kusama's 2021 MONA exhibition, constructed with paper cut-outs and masking tape. An expression of form through restraint. Using minimal elements to communicate maximum expression. Typographic treatment is used as a compositional tool to explore space, rhythm, structure and visual weight.
