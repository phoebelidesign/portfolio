Wearable

A wearable publication; a wire crochet glove functioning as a book, formed in response to my film Waking Moment (2023). It explores temporary states of stillness - moments outside of time, personal and difficult to define. Constructed to fit my own hand using 26 and 28 gauge silver wire, it can only be read through touch. Memo glove serves as both vessel and narrative: the missing index finger gestures towards the vulnerability of moments of clarity; the curved soine requires a turn of the hand to read, referencing a moment of self-reflection in the film. It must be worn to be read, completed by the hand that holds it.
