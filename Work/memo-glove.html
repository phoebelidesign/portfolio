MEMO GLOVE

Next: Sunburn
Previous: Waking Moment

Publication
Wearable

A wire crochet glove functioning as a book, formed in response to my film Waking Moment (2023). An exploration of temporary states of stillness - moments outside of time, personal and difficult to define. Constructed to fit my own hand using 26 and 28 gauge silver wire. Memo glove serves as both vessel and narrative; it must be worn in order to be read, its story provided by the hand that holds it.
